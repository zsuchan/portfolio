About Me

I'm a Software Developer specializing in OutSystems with over 9 years of experience. I work on internal enterprise systems — the kind that real teams depend on every day for their operations.

Most of my work involves backend logic and system integration. That includes designing webhook flows, connecting APIs across internal and external systems, and making sure data stays consistent even when things go wrong.

Experienced in handling edge cases such as duplicate transactions, data inconsistency, and concurrent requests in real-world systems.

I've spent a lot of time in production environments — investigating issues, fixing root causes, and improving system stability. I prefer solving problems properly rather than patching them quickly.
